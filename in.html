About IEDC

What is IEDC?

IEDC is a platform to provide students an opportunity to experiment and innovate.The IEDCs are established in academic institutions across Kerala having requisite expertise and infrastructure. The main objective is to create an "entrepreneur" , who has the capacity and willingness to develop, organize and manage a business venture along with any of its risks in order to make a profit. To promote this the state Government has already sanctioned a very big fund to Kerala Start up Mission and Start up Villages which are the nodal agencies for incubation, guidance and mentoring.

The IEDC Philosophy

The concept of Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Cell (IEDC) was formulated to promote innovation and entrepreneurial culture in educational institutions and to develop institutional mechanism to foster techno-entrepreneurship for generation of wealth and employment by persons. It basically inculcates personality development through creation and participation. The participation has two angles, one is personal and the other is social. In participating the IEDC activities one can bridge the skill gaps, build their own portfolio and also build their leadership qualities. By developing a feasible and economically viable solution one can bring the cultural change in the society so that the quality of life index can be improved. Through these activities the members are leading to mentorship, community access and high profile interactions.

IEDC@ICET

The IEDC@ICET is one of the IEDC cells all over Kerala, affiliated to the Kerala Startup Mission and was inaugurated by honourable chief minister Sri. Oommen Chandy on October 18th 2014 by unveiling the IEDC logo. The IEDC of ICET is headed by Dr. M Jamal Muhammed Zubair who is the Nodal Officer.This centre provides financial support, mentoring and Infrastructural support for developing innovative products.Our main objective is to create an entrepreneurship eco-system in the college, where students would learn the technicalities of entrepreneurship and become job providers instead of job seekers.For that we are conducting a lot of activities like the following:

DYUTHI-2K16

DYUTHI-2K16 is an energy conservation project initiated by Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology with the Paipra Panchayat .This process is mainly focused to create awareness among public to use the LED bulbs to conserve electrical energy. LED bulbs are made by the Bootcamp students of the college and distributed to the public. Hence the aim of Dyuthi 2k16 is to totally convert the Paipra Grama Panchayat to use the LED bulbs.
